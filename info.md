## About Ecolive

Ecolive is a sustainability-focused organization operating across India. They specialize in ESG consulting, water conservation, and CSR program execution. Their approach encompasses avoiding harmful chemicals and plastics, promoting recycling, tree-planting initiatives, naturopathy/yoga adoption, and organic food products — all designed to elevate corporate brand value while fostering environmental sensitivity.

## Services & Programs

### 1. Water Conservation

Ecolive addresses major gaps in rainwater harvesting, groundwater recharge, and water conservation. Their framework uses the acronym **WATER**:

- **W** — Water Avoidance Through Efficiency & Reduce
- **A** — Availability Through Efficient Reuse
- **T** — (Wa)ter Augmentation Through Effective Recycling
- **E** — Enhanced Recharge

Core approach: **Reduce, Reuse, Recycle & Recharge**

Services include rainwater harvesting, groundwater recharge, and water conservation projects tailored to customer location and profile.

### 2. ESG Solutions — ECHO Framework

Ecolive's proprietary ESG framework is called **ECHO** (Ecologically Conscious & Happy Organisation). ECHO ensures comprehensive strategy, planning, implementation, and monitoring across:

- Water
- Waste
- Energy
- Air
- Soil
- Plants
- Employee Health

ECHO accords equal priority to ESG score — equivalent to top-line or bottom-line financial metrics.

**ESG Services:**

- **ESG Consultation** — Gap analysis around water, waste, and energy management; remedial measures implementation; plantation drives with site identification and employee engagement.
- **Waste Management** — Practical, easy-to-implement, effective, and affordable waste solutions.
- **Clean Energy Solutions** — Solar energy products and solutions for homes and offices.

### 3. CSR Programs

Ecolive provides end-to-end CSR campaign planning and implementation across India:

- Beneficiary identification
- Project conceptualization through completion and handover
- Multi-sector ecology projects
- Awareness campaigns and sensitization initiatives
- Project reporting with measurable outcomes

**Sample CSR Projects:**

- Solar street lights and rooftop solar installations for villages/schools
- Rainwater harvesting systems
- Electric vehicle charging infrastructure
- Cloth bag distribution initiatives
- Book and computer donations to underserved communities
- Green belt maintenance
