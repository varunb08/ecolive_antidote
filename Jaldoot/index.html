Giving Water a Way Back to the Earth.

JalDOOT is a citizen-led movement transforming water scarcity into water security. We train local stewards to bridge the skill gap in rainwater harvesting, one village at a time.

Closing the Knowledge Chasm

While billions are spent on infrastructure, the expertise to maintain and build local rainwater systems is disappearing. Rural India faces a critical **Skill Gap** in sustainable water management.
